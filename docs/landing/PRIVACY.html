Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

TL;DR: Your conversations stay on your device. We don't see them, store them, or sell them. Period.

What Phloem Does

Phloem captures your AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini and stores them locally on your device. This allows you to search and recall past conversations without relying on each platform's limited history.

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Your conversations - Never sent to our servers
  • Your prompts or AI responses - Stay on your device
  • Personal information - We don't ask for it
  • API keys - Never transmitted
  • File contents - Not accessed
  • Browsing history - Not tracked

Data We MAY Collect (With Your Consent)

If you opt-in to telemetry, we collect anonymous operational metrics only:

  • Event types (e.g., "memory_saved", "search_performed")
  • Timestamps
  • Aggregate counts
  • Error types (not content)
  • App version

We NEVER collect content, PII, or anything that could identify you or your conversations.

You control telemetry:
phloem telemetry show - See exactly what we collect
phloem telemetry disable - Turn it off completely

Data Storage

  • Free tier: All data stored locally in ~/.phloem/

Third-Party Services

  • GitHub: Hosts our open-source code for transparency.

Data Deletion

Your data, your choice:

  • phloem export - Export all your data
  • phloem clear - Delete all local data
  • Uninstall the app - All data is removed

Open Source

Phloem is open source. You can audit the code yourself to verify our privacy claims.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of significant changes via the app and update this page.
